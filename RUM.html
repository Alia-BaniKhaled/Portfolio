About My Project

My "Rum" collection is inspired by the beauty of Wadi Rum's desert and the authentic life of the Bedouins with their rich traditions. Each piece in this collection resonates with the spirit and culture of the Jordanian Bedouins, carrying the essence of their history and the beauty of nature. Through my designs, I strive to depict the vast desert landscapes, where golden sands intertwine with the patterns of Bedouin Sadu (the fabric used in their tents and furnishings), reflecting the simplicity and elegance of Bedouin attire. This collection blends tradition with innovation, evoking ancient tales and merging them with modernity, creating clothes that narrate stories of resilience and beauty, capturing the essence of Bedouin life with a contemporaryÂ spirit.
